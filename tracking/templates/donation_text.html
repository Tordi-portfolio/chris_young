Where Your Help Goes

From the Pacific islands to the Amazon rainforest, Jason has personally led humanitarian aid efforts. Through his organization, the 222 Foundation, he has donated thousands of gallons of clean water, funded children's cancer treatments, and planted thousands of trees to fight deforestation.
