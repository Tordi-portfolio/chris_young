All About Jason Momoa
Jason Momoa is more than a Hollywood name—he’s a creative force of nature. Known for his legendary roles as Aquaman and Khal Drogo, Jason brings strength, vulnerability, and authenticity to every screen he steps onto. But his story doesn’t stop at the surface.
As a producer, director, and writer, Jason is deeply involved in shaping the stories he tells. He founded Pride of Gypsies, a production company focused on soulful, untamed, and visually arresting work. He doesn’t just act—he crafts experiences.
When he's not on set, Jason is in the wild—rock climbing, surfing, building motorcycles, or sitting in the dirt with his children. His soul belongs to nature, and he’s a vocal advocate for clean oceans, sustainable living, and Indigenous rights.
Family is everything. Culture is everything. Jason proudly honors his Native Hawaiian roots, and raises his kids with values of respect, earth connection, and fearlessness. He’s not trying to be perfect—he’s trying to be real.
Whether he’s sketching a tattoo design, building a custom bike, filming a documentary, or hugging a tree, Jason lives a life of fire, creation, and love. His work is bold. His legacy is rising. And through it all, he stays grounded by his people, his passions, and his purpose.
"I want to use my life to protect the things I love—my family, my culture, and this earth. That’s the legacy I care about.”
— Jason Momoa
